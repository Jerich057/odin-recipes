Ice Cubes

One of the greatest summer recipe hits include this incredibly refreshing substance know as ice, This recipe although complex is one of the better ones on this recipe website.

This can go into anything put it into your drink, put it in your mouth, put it in your ass, anything is possible with ice the only limit is how creative you are

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Cocaine
  • Ice cube tray

Steps

  1. Grab some water can be bottled or from sink
  2. Pour the water over the ice cube tray make sure they are properly filled
  3. Once the tray is filled sprinkle a bit of coke over the tray for flavor
  4. Shove the ice cube tray in the freezer
  5. Wait a couple hours then take out of freezer
  6. Pop ice cubes from the tray and enjoy
