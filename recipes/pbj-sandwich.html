PB&J Sandwich

This recipe is a classic, the peanut butter and jelly sandwich is the easiest to make and the superior sandwich compared to others, you can even be creative and add extras in the middle like honey or bacon or you can be a social outcast and add a pickle

Ingredients

  • 2 slices or bread
  • Peanut butter
  • Strawberry or grape jam
  • Love and passion(maybe add that pickle you weirdo)

Steps

  1. Put both slices of bread on a plate
  2. Slather jam on one piece of bread and another with peanut butter
  3. Put the pickle on one side of the bread
  4. Combine and enjoy the food of gods
